The Process


I started Books with Boba in order to keep myself from hoarding all of the books I read, but also to promote literature from Asian American and Asian writers both within and beyond mainstream recognition. All of these books are available for lending, unless listed otherwise. Though I fully support public libraries, being able to hand off some of my favorite books to my friends is something I already do -- I figured I should formalize the process regardless!

If you're interested in borrowing a book, take a look at the books listed below and find one that piques your interest. Then, go to the bottom of the page and fill out the book request form. If the book is available, I'll send it your way.

Take as long as you'd like with the book (chances are, if I really like it I will most likely get a personal copy). When you're done, either send it to me or pass it on to another person! Knowledge and representation are both extremely powerful. Happy reading!
