After realizing that I haven't done a very good job at commendig myself for a job well done throughout college (and all parts of my academic and professional career before then), I resolved to start doing that with each major step in my young adult life, and beyond! Therefore, I am marking each of my professional accomplishments with a Southeast Asian pastry from a small business, because representation matters.

If you have a pastry that you think I should queue up for my next one, send me a note and I'll make sure to try it in the future!
